OUR SERVICES

BUNKERS

Offshore from India, we are delivering safer, faster and more efficient refueling services than ever before - meeting the needs of oil rig operators, container and tanker fleets as well as local fishermen.

FUELS

We supply refined marine fuels to vessels including container ships, dry bulk carriers, cruise ships, oil tankers, and ferries, both in port and at sea.

INDUSTRIAL SUPPLIES

We serve our clients with an optimum quality of all grades of fuels and Lubricants for all types of industries across the country. Our range of products is developed from high grade raw material with most competitive prices.

TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

We provide wide range of transportation services (Trucking and Barging services) for bunker supply at east coast and west coast of India. Transportation services can be utilised for refuelling ocean-going vessels or fixed structures in deep water.
